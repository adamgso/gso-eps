Product Description

Whether you are a student, teacher, composer, or play out professionally, Emotional Pitch® is your key to reaching the next level of musicianship quickly and easily. With instructional materials that were developed with all levels and types of musicians in mind, this course spotlights the connection between the most basic human emotions and music, and lights the way so you are able to focus on the art—and not the science—of music theory.

The lessons taught in Emotional Pitch® start with the essential, core ideas of theory. You'll then go on an artistic journey into a world where music and emotions unite. Learn what moves audiences to the edge of their seats, why certain songs evoke particular emotions inside of you, and how music theory truly does come in handy when you want to play or compose what you hear and feel.
